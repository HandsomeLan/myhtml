About / 简介

我是厦门大学管理学院中国能源政策研究院的博士研究生（硕博连读），师从林伯强教授。 我的研究领域主要涵盖能源经济、公司金融与资产定价，关注能源价格波动对宏观经济与微观企业行为的影响。

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the China Institute for Studies in Energy Policy, School of Management, Xiamen University, under the supervision of Prof. Boqiang Lin. My research interests include Energy Economics, Corporate Finance, and Asset Pricing, with a focus on the impacts of energy price fluctuations on macroeconomic and firm-level outcomes.
